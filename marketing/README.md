## Brand essentials (use everywhere)

- **Name:** ProCabinet.App (the `.App` is amber `#e8a838` when on dark; black when on light)
- **Tagline:** *Cabinetry Production Dashboard* (formal) or *Workshop OS for cabinetmakers* (marketing)
- **Brand colour:** amber `#e8a838` on near-black `#111111`
- **Default voice:** British English, peer-to-peer trade voice, founder-led
- **Profile bio (universal):** *"Workshop OS for cabinetmakers. Quote, cut, schedule, bill — one tool. Built by a maker. Free to try ↓"*

## Pricing (use these numbers exactly)

- **Free** to start — 5 of each (clients, cabinets, quotes, orders, cut lists)

- **$299/yr** annual Pro (save 29% vs monthly)

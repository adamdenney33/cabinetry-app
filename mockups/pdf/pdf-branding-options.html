ProCabinet — PDF branding options

Three branding intensities for free-tier PDFs. Each card shows free (left) vs Pro (right). Pick one.
The header on every card uses the new layout: logo top-left (when uploaded) with caption below in the order Business name → Address → Phone → Email. The right side keeps the document title (QUOTATION / TAX INVOICE / etc.). The branding choice only affects the footer / page chrome, not the header.
1 Subtle footer A single 6.5pt grey line — same size as today, just rephrased. Minimal visual impact, doesn't compete with your branding.

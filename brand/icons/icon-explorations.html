Icon Mark Explorations

ProCabinet.App

Ten dynamic treatments of the cabinet mark to sit alongside the wordmark. Static options are brand-safe everywhere; animated and interactive ones add life for web, app icons and motion contexts.

Preview surface
Hover option 9 to see the 3D tilt react.
01

Brand Solid

Static

The safe default. Flat amber squircle, white glyph. Reads cleanly at any size and on any background.

02

Warm Gradient

Static

Amber tile with a soft top-light gradient and inner highlight — a touch of depth without animation.

03

Duotone

Static

Glyph filled with the full brand spectrum, amber into teal. Strongest on dark surfaces.

04

Conic Shimmer

Animated

A metallic conic gradient rotates slowly through the amber range — like brushed brass catching light.

05

Gloss Sweep

Animated

A specular highlight glides across the white glyph on a loop. Premium, glossy feel.

06

Aurora Glow

Animated

Glyph-only mark on near-black with a breathing amber glow. Great for splash screens and hero loops.

07

Glassmorphism

Animated

Frosted glass tile over drifting amber/teal blobs, with a translucent white glyph. Modern and airy.

08

Embossed

Static

Tone-on-tone glyph pressed into the amber surface with a subtle bevel. Tactile, understated.

09

3D Tilt

Interactive

A floating, perspective-tilted tile with a raised glyph. Reacts on hover — ideal for a landing hero.

10

Mesh Motion

Animated

A living gradient mesh of amber, gold and teal flows inside the glyph. Colourful and energetic.

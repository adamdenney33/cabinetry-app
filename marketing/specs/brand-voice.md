# ProCabinet.App — Brand voice &amp; founder story

A single-source-of-truth Claude can re-read for any future copy run. Keeps the voice consistent across posts, DMs, ads, emails and PR.

## Founder story (use as origin in every long-form piece)

Adam ran his own bespoke cabinetry workshop for over 10 years (canonical bio claim — decision 2026-07-03; keep every byline, author schema and about-blurb on this exact framing). The business ran on a stack of spreadsheets and disconnected apps for pricing, quoting, invoicing, scheduling and cut lists.

Over those years he built his own quoting system — and it changed the business. Quote turnaround dropped from **hours to minutes**. More importantly, it became simple enough to **hand off to an assistant**, freeing Adam up for the parts he actually loved: design, the workshop, talking to clients.

When the shop closed, the quoting system became the seed of ProCabinet.App. Adam rebuilt it as a tool anyone can use, then bolted on every other module he wished he'd had at the time:

- A **cabinet library with linked cut lists** he could only have dreamed of
- An **auto-updating visual schedule** — the scratch he could never itch
- **Always-current stock tracking** — the silent time-sink, finally fixed

It's deliberately simple and intuitive. Using the app should make you understand your business better and quietly enforce the workflows that make a shop profitable.

This is just the beginning — more integrations and features are on the roadmap.

## Voice rules

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Sound like a tradesperson talking to other tradespeople | Sound like a SaaS marketing team |
| Lead with shop reality (sheet waste, late quotes, missed orders) | Lead with feature names |
| Use real numbers from the app (mm, sheets, $X) | Use vague claims ("save time", "boost productivity") |
| Show tools earned the right to recommend the app | Pitch before contributing |
| British English in default copy ("optimise", "colour", "labour") | Mixed spelling — pick a region per channel and stick to it |
| Mention the free tier (5 of each) as the entry point | Lead with the paid plan |
| Use the founder voice ("I built this because…") | Use corporate "we" unless you have a team |
| Keep posts short and concrete | Long generic copy |

## Phrases that work

- *"Built by a maker, for makers."*
- *"Quote, cut, schedule and bill from one place."*
- *"Replace your spreadsheets, notebooks and post-its."*
- *"From hours to minutes."*
- *"The workshop OS for cabinetmakers."*
- *"Run a 1–10 person cabinet shop? This is for you."*

## Phrases to avoid

- "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "next-gen"
- "Unlock your potential"
- "Streamline your workflow"
- "Powered by AI"
- "All-in-one platform" (use "one tool to…" instead)
- "Synergy", "leverage", "ecosystem"

## Pricing always framed as

- **Free** to start (5 of each — clients, cabinets, quotes, orders, cut lists)
- **$35/mo** monthly Pro
- **$299/yr** annual Pro (save 29%, effective ~$25/mo)
- **$299 once** Founders' Lifetime (capped at 50, launch week only) — *"same price as one annual, used forever"*

## App-specific facts to anchor copy in

- 8 tabs: Dashboard, Clients, Quote, Cabinet, Cut List, Stock, Orders, Schedule
- Cabinet Library with reusable templates → drop into any quote
- My Rates: change a rate, every quote re-prices live
- Cut List optimiser: respects grain direction, kerf, nests for minimum waste
- Auto-Gantt schedule: respects working hours, weekends, daily capacity
- 5-stage order pipeline (Draft → Sent → Approved → Order → Production → Complete)
- Live stock tracking with low-stock alerts
- One-click PDF exports: branded quotes, job sheets, delivery notes, workshop cut diagrams
- Sample data on first login: "Smith Kitchen Renovation" — $8,450 job, 3 cabinet types, real cut list

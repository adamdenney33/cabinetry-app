Who it's for

The audience that converts is the one already watching someone build. If your viewers make things for a living, or want to, they're the fit.

Trade YouTubers. Cabinet and kitchen build channels, workshop vlogs — long-form gives room to actually show a quote come together.
Reel-first makers. Furniture and joinery accounts on Instagram and TikTok, where a 60-second "watch me price this" clip does the work.
Trade educators. People teaching others to start a cabinet business — their audience is new shops, which is exactly who needs pricing sorted early.
Tool and workshop reviewers. Software slots naturally into a shop-setup or business-side video, alongside the saws and the CNC.

The pitch is the product

Most cabinet-making channels show the build. Almost none show the quoting, the costing, the admin that decides whether the job made money — which is the part their audience quietly struggles with most. That gap is the content.

The live quote link is the thing worth filming: a customer opens a page on their phone, ticks options in or out, watches the total update, signs off and pays a deposit. Screenshots and the demo film from the home page are there to build from, so a good clip takes an afternoon rather than a weekend. Common questions

When do commissions get paid?

When a referral pays their first Pro invoice — not on signup, and not during the free trial. There's a 30-day hold to cover refunds and chargebacks, then the commission is payable.

How long does the referral stay tracked?

The tracking cookie lasts 60 days from the click — long enough to cover the 14-day Pro trial and a decision after it. A referral code also credits you when someone hears about the tool in a video and signs up later, which is why it's worth pushing the code as hard as the link.

How long do I earn on each customer?

Twelve months from a referral's first payment. On monthly plans that's 30% every month for a year; on annual and one-off plans it's 30% of the single invoice. After twelve months the commission ends, whether or not the customer stays.

How and when am I paid?

Payouts run monthly through the affiliate dashboard, once your cleared balance is over the $50 minimum. The dashboard shows exactly what's pending, what's cleared and what's been paid.

Do I have to be a paying customer?

No. You need a free account so your link and dashboard have somewhere to live, and every account starts with 14 days of Pro — enough to actually try the thing you're recommending. There's no cost to join and no cost to stay in the program.

What are the rules?

Disclose the paid relationship — the FTC in the US and the ASA in the UK both require it, and it protects you as much as anyone. Don't bid on ProCabinet's name in your own paid ads, and don't refer yourself or your own account. Beyond that, recommend it however suits your channel.

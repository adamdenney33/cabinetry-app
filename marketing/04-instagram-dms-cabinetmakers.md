# Instagram DMs to cabinet makers — 3 options

> **Goal:** turn an Instagram scroll into a free-trial signup or a demo booking.
> **Volume:** aim for 10–20 personalised DMs per day in the launch week. Quality &gt; quantity — a generic DM is worse than no DM.
> **Always:** spend 30 seconds on their profile first. Mention something specific from their last 1–2 posts. If you can't find something specific, don't message them.

## Rules of the game

1. **Never start with "Hi I hope you're well"** — instant delete. Open with their work.
2. **Mention something specific** in their last 1–2 posts. If you can't, skip them.
3. **One paragraph max in the opener.** They're scrolling.
4. **Soft CTA** ("happy to send a Loom" / "free to spin up an account"). Hard sell on first DM = blocked.
5. **One follow-up only** if they don't reply. Send 3–4 days later, even shorter:
   > Hey {name} — no pressure, just bumping this up the inbox in case it got buried. Either way, the {recent post you'd seen by then} is great.
6. **Track each DM in your CRM** (Notion / Airtable / HubSpot). Columns: handle, posted, replied, signed up, paid.
7. **Don't DM the same person from multiple platforms** in the same week — looks desperate.

## DM finder workflow (where to source 100 cabinet maker IGs in 2 hours)

1. **Hashtag mining:** scroll #cabinetmaker, #cabinetry, #customcabinets, #joinery, #cabinetshop, #builtins, #wardrobedesign, #customkitchens. Open every account that's clearly a working shop (not a hobbyist).
2. **Geo-tags:** search by city — "Manchester cabinetmaker", "Brisbane joinery", "Toronto custom cabinets".
3. **Followers of competitors:** open Cabinet Vision's IG, Mozaik's IG — many followers are cabinet shops.
4. **Cross-references:** when you find one shop, check who they follow + tag.
5. Save handles into a Notion table with columns: handle, location, shop size estimate, last post date, what to mention, sent, replied.

## Daily DM rhythm

- **15 in the morning** (during their first cuppa, ~8–9am their local time)
- **5–10 in the evening** (after dinner, when they're scrolling)
- **None at the weekend** for the cold first DM — wait until Monday morning. Saturday is for replies and follow-ups only.

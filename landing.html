Custom Cabinet Quote Builder

Build bespoke cabinet quotes in seconds. Set your rates and times for each production step once, then let the builder do the maths.

  • Pull materials, edgebanding, hardware & finishes straight from your stock library.
  • Save reusable cabinets to your library, with cut-list parts linked ready to drop into new jobs.
  • Delegate quoting with confidence — someone else can handle the admin with very little training.

Auto-Schedule Production Calendar

Production that schedules itself. Set your available working hours and a priority for each order — work is allocated automatically, in line or concurrently.

  • Add extra time when a job runs over, and track exactly where you're losing money.
  • Manual overrides for fixed start dates or set durations — auto-schedule works around them.

Stock Library

Every material, tracked — and fed straight into quotes, cut lists and orders.

  • Set a low-stock alert quantity for each item individually.
  • Total stock values make your end-of-year accounts easy.
  • Deduct from stock as you build, so on-hand figures stay honest.

Cut List Optimiser

Cut sheets that don't waste board. Pull panels and edge banding from your stock library and let the optimiser nest them.

  • Deduct from stock with one button.
  • Save reusable parts for repeat cabinets.
  • Set grain direction for individual parts and panels.
  • Add edge banding to specific sides, with the trim toggle set to each band's thickness.

Quotes, Orders & Clients

From first quote to final invoice, on one pipeline.

  • Create quotes and invoices with times linked directly to the schedule.
  • Build quotes from cabinets, standard line items and stock from your library.
  • Convert quotes to orders and track every stage on a visual pipeline.
  • Export PDFs for customers and for the workshop floor.
  • See every quote and order for a client in one place.

Business Dashboard

Your whole business on one screen. Pull all your data together for a top-level view.

  • Track quotes, orders and revenue.
  • See your next 7 days of production at a glance.
  • Low-stock alerts always visible.
  • Create any new entry with one click.

It's not just an app. It's your workshop's operating system.

Set your rates, times, stock and process once — ProCabinet runs the same repeatable system on every job. The SOPs are built in, so quoting, cutting, scheduling and billing happen the same way every time, whoever's at the keyboard.

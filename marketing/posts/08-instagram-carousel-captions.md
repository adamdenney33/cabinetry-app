## POST 2 — Cut List: connected to stock & saved cabinets
**Design:** Cut List Optimiser (6 slides)

You've probably already got cut list software. Here's what it isn't doing.

Most optimisers nest your panels and stop there — so you still build the cut list by hand on every job. ProCabinet's cut list is connected to the rest of your shop, so the work is only done once:

· Save a cabinet and its cut list travels with it — drop it into any job, no re-entry
· Pull panels and edge banding straight from your stock library
· Nest onto the fewest sheets, with grain and kerf respected
· Cut, then deduct from stock in one click — no separate sheet to update after

Your cabinets, quotes, cut lists and stock all stay in sync from a single entry. On repeat work, that's hours saved every week.
